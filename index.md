# Privacy Policy for Wizard Trick Tracker

**Last updated: April 7, 2026**

## Overview

Wizard Trick Tracker is a free, ad-free scorekeeper app for the Wizard card game. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

This app does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data (scores, player names, preferences) is stored locally on your device only.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Internet Access

This app does not require an internet connection to function. No data is sent to any server.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
